Chum

2 oz jellyfish squeezings

1 seahorse snout

1/2 cup whale blubber

1 sprinkle anchor rust

1 old sock

1 banana peel

1 bucket trash

1 recently used napkin

  1. Dice the seahorse snout.
  2. Melt the whale blubber.
  3. Mix ingredients in a recently cleaned toilet.
  4. Close the seat and flush to mix.
  5. Plate and sprinkle with anchor rust.
